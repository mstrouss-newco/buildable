Your games, your rules

Keep it private or publish
to the buildable arcade.

Every game you build lives in your space. You decide who gets to play it.

🌍
Public
Publish your game to the buildable arcade. Other kids can play it, heart it, and leave comments. See how many plays and hearts you can rack up.
  • Visible to the buildable community
  • Earns hearts, plays, and ratings
  • Appears in the public arcade
  • Parents review before publishing
🔒
Private
Keep your game just for you and your family. Share it with specific people via a link. Great for gifts, class projects, or games still in progress.
  • Only visible to you and your family
  • Share with anyone via private link
  • Share with your class or teacher
  • Switch to public any time
