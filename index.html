For parents & educators

Built for their imagination.
Designed for your peace of mind.

Buildable is not an open AI tool. It's a structured creative environment where kids build, not browse. No open chat, no rabbit holes. Just creation — guided, safe, and age-appropriate.

🛡️
Structured AI, not open chat
Kids describe creative ideas. The AI builds them. There's no freeform conversation, no access to the open web, no surprises.
📊
Weekly progress reports
Every subject practiced, every challenge answered, every level reached — summarized clearly every week.
🎚️
You control the difficulty
Set challenges to Easy, Medium, or Hard per child. Change it anytime. Their progress adapts with them.
✅
You review before they publish
Nothing goes public without your approval. Kids keep building; you stay in control of what the world sees.

Their world, their rules

Publish to the buildable arcade. Other kids can play it, heart it, and discover it. Watch their creation reach players worldwide.

  • Earns hearts, plays, and ratings

🔒
Private
Keep it just for your family. Share a private link with anyone you choose. Perfect for gifts, class projects, or works in progress.
  • Only you and your family can see it
  • Share privately with anyone via link
  • Share with class or teacher only
  • Go public whenever they're ready
